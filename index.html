TINY RESTAURANT

COOK • PLAY • EARN

This is the first of its kind NFT cooking game inspired by “ Cooking Mama” where you will be rewarded with $TNYR depending on your cooking skills

GAMEPLAY

TinyRestaurant is inspired by the famous cooking simulation videogame saga “Cooking Mama”, where you had to show your cooking skills through a set of minigames and get the highest score. Now in TinyRestaurant you will have to put on the chef’s hat again and impress all your clients with the most delicious meals, all this in order to obtain great rewards.

However, and unlike in the Cooking Mama series, in TinyRestaurant you will have to get a cookware kit and take care of keeping your refrigerator full of food in order to be able to cook and advance on the Global Rank until one day you become a Corporate Chef and become able to own your own chain of restaurants.

Restaurants Presale
It will be done in the next 24 hours after completing the whitelist phase. A total of 2,500 Restaurants will be sold at a price of 200 BUSD per Restaurant. At least one Restaurant per whitelist member is guaranteed. This sale will end after 1 hour or Restaurants have sold out.
If there are less than 2,500 users on the whitelist, this pre-sale time will be divided into two modules. A maximum of 1 Restaurant per user can be purchased in each module. There will be a 5 minutes wait time between modules. In case there are restaurants left unpurchased, they will be reserved to be sold in the public sale.

Cookware Kits Presale
It will be done in the next 24 hours after completing Restaurants pre-sale. A total of 3,000 Cookware Kits will be sold at a price of 50 BUSD per Cookware Kit. At least one Cookware Kit per whitelist member is guaranteed. This sale will end after 1 hour or Cookware Kits have sold out.
If there are less than 2,500 users on the whitelist, this pre-sale time will be divided into two modules. In the first module, a maximum of 1 Cookware Kit per user can be purchased. In the second module you can buy a maximum of 2 Kitchen Kits per user can be purchased. There will be a 5 minute wait time between modules. If there are Cookware Kits left unpurchased, they will be reserved to be sold in the public sale.

TNYR Private Sale
It will be done in the next 24 hours after completing Cookware Kits pre-sale. A total of 500,000 TNYR will be sold at a price of 0,6 BUSD per TNYR. This sale will end after 3 hours or TNYR have sold out.
The minimum purchase will be 25 TNYR and the maximum purchase will be 350 TNYR. In case all the TNYR tokens are not sold within the estimated period, a second round will be held in which people who have bought at least 100 TNR will participate. If TNYR is left unpurchased, they will be reserved for sale in the public sale round or burned (yet to be decided).

TNYR Public Pre-sale
It will be done in the next 24 hours after completing TNYR Private Sale. A total of 900,000 TNYR will be sold at a price of 0,8 BUSD per TNYR. This sale will end after 3 hours have or TNYR have sold out.
The minimum purchase will be 10 TNYR and the maximum purchase will be 450 TNYR. In case all the TNYR tokens are not sold within the estimated period, a second round will be held. If TNYR is left unpurchased, they will be added to the reward pool or burned (yet to be decided).
The token will be listed at the end of Public Pre-sale at a price of $1,2 per token.

P2E
In this phase, new Cookware Kits can be minted, or freely bought and sold through the marketplace. The start of play to earn is planned 5 days after the end of all the previous phases.
